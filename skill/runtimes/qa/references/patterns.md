## Acceptance Matrix

Map every accepted criterion to at least one scenario or state why it cannot be exercised.

## UX Quality Pattern

For user-facing work, inspect:

- whether the primary action is understandable;
- feedback during waiting and completion;
- error recovery;
- content clarity;
- keyboard, focus, contrast, and responsive behavior when relevant;
- consistency with accepted Design Strategy.

## Scope Pattern

Check both directions:

- promised behavior missing;
- unaccepted behavior added.

Either can be a product coherence failure.
