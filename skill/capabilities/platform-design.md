# Platform Design Capability

## Purpose

Adapt accepted interface decisions to the delivery surfaces and technology stack selected by Architecture.

## Procedure

1. Read the accepted Architecture and Standards Profile.
2. Resolve each delivery surface to Web, Mobile, Desktop, or another Architecture-defined platform family.
3. Load only the applicable platform reference and accepted stack adapter.
4. Define platform navigation, input, layout, system integration, accessibility, and lifecycle behavior that affects the interface.
5. Separate shared product intent from deliberate platform variants.
6. Route a required technology change to Architecture instead of changing the stack.

## Evidence

- accepted stack and delivery-surface reference;
- applicable platform guidance;
- shared and platform-specific decisions;
- unresolved adapter limitations;
- constraints passed to Development and QA.
