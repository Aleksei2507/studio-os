# Product QA Capability

## Purpose

Evaluate the working increment as a product against accepted scenarios and quality expectations.

## Procedure

1. Derive QA scenarios from Project Brief, Work Item acceptance criteria, and accepted Interface Design when available.
2. Confirm technical Validation is sufficient to begin QA.
3. Load QA-relevant rules from the Project Standards Profile.
4. Exercise user-visible flows through browser, API, CLI, device, or another relevant interface.
5. Check errors, edge cases, scope coherence, accessibility, privacy, and UX where applicable.
6. Record evidence and severity for every finding.

## Evidence

- Scenario-by-scenario result.
- Screenshots, outputs, or observations when relevant.
- Applicable standards coverage.
- Interface Design conformance when applicable.
- PASS, CONCERNS, or FAIL decision.
- Release blockers and residual risks.
