  - compares source dates, populations, definitions, and methodology
  - records the contradiction rather than selecting convenient evidence
  - lowers confidence or recommends more research when the decision remains uncertain
tags: [research, evidence, uncertainty]

# Scenario

Two credible sources report different conclusions because they study different geographies and company sizes.

Research should explain the mismatch and its decision impact. It must not silently choose the conclusion that supports the original idea.
